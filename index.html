UI/UX STUDY

Peer Play:

Music Player App

ABOUT PEER PLAY

PeerPlay music player is an app which understands our taste by suggesting the right kind of music based off previous search from the vast ocean of songs, genres, artists and more. It does this well with a colourful user interface.

PROBLEM

Design a music player app, to curate and cater to the user's specific tastes through its algorithm and interface, and to make sure that its machine learning functions improve over time and with frequency of usage.

SOLUTIONS

During onboarding, the app can ask questions to know the user taste in music so that it can give appropriate suggestions. According to the user behavior, we can prioritize the content and make it clearly visible for the user to select. Otherwise, users face the problem of choice overload and eventually lose interest in using the app. Making the lyrics available for all songs uploaded on the app.

RESEARCH

Adopted one of the primary methods for conducting the user research- user surveys, with a survey size of 10. The motive behind selecting research method was to get a quantitative data to identify the user tastes, behavior and preferences. The survey form which I prepared covered a range of questions. Some of the relevant questions are listed below:

  • Q1 Which apps do you use to listen to music?
  • Q2 Do you like to explore new music or are you happy listening to your regular playlist?
  • Q3 How do you discover new music?
  • Q4 Do you listen to suggested songs from a particular music app?
  • Q5 Do you create your own playlist?
  • Q6 How often do you share music with others?
  • Q7 Do you prefer the Dark or Light theme in our music player app?
